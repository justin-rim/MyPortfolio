About me

Having spent the past 7 years working in retail, logistics, and in commercial coffee equipment, these jobs could not scratch the itch for creative, challenging, and satisfying work. Having been affected by COVID-19 through a layoff, I chose to put my focus into something I had always been afraid to go into: programming.

Programming, an interest of mine since my Intro to Python course at UC Irvine, always bothered me. It seemed so difficult, yet almost insultingly easy once the solution was revealed through another person. Coding problems continue to reveal themselves as more diffcult, but I've come to appreciate the rigor, along with the satisfaction of finding my solution.

My current stack includes: HTML, CSS, Javascript, Bootstrap, React, NPM, Node.js, Express.js, and PostgreSQL.
